Who's behind the camera

A small studio,
on purpose.

AB Photo Shots is owner-operated — the person you talk to is the person who shows up, shoots your day, and edits every frame.

Weddings are all I photograph. Not headshots on Tuesday and products on Thursday — just couples, ceremonies, and the small unrepeatable moments in between. That focus is the whole point of keeping this studio small: you get one photographer's full attention from first message to final album, not a rotating crew from a big commercial operation.

The style leans candid and unhurried. Some portraits, yes — but mostly your day as it actually happened: your grandmother laughing, your hands shaking during the vows, the last dance nobody staged.

What I photograph

Your day, told in order.

Instead of a menu of packages, here's how coverage actually unfolds — from the yes to the last dance. Every engagement is scoped one-on-one, because no two days run alike.

  1. The engagement session — before the day

    A relaxed session, somewhere that means something to you two. It's how we get comfortable together — so on the wedding day the camera already feels like a friend, not a stranger.

    Engagement Sessions

  2. Full wedding day coverage — getting ready to goodnight

    Documentary-first coverage of the whole day: the quiet nerves in the morning, the ceremony, the toasts, the dance floor once the candles are low. I stay present and out of the way.

    Wedding Day Photography

  3. Bridal & couple portraits — the frames on the wall

    A carved-out stretch of golden light, just the two of you. Gently directed, never stiff — these are the images your family will ask for prints of.

    Bridal / Couple Portraits

  4. Elopements & small ceremonies — just as sacred

    Marrying at a courthouse, in a backyard, or with ten guests instead of two hundred? A small studio is built for small weddings. Reach out and we'll shape coverage around it.

    Intimate Coverage

How it works

Unhurried, start to finish.

one

Say hello

Call or message with your date and where you're celebrating. You'll hear back from me directly — there's no front desk here.

two

A real conversation

We sit down — in person or over a call — and talk through your day, your people, and what matters most in the photos. Then I put together coverage that fits, plainly priced.

three

Your date, reserved

Once you book, that date is yours alone. I only take one wedding per day, so the couple in front of my camera always has all of me.

four

The album arrives

Every frame is edited by the same eyes that shot it — warm, true-to-the-moment tones, delivered as a gallery you'll keep coming back to.
